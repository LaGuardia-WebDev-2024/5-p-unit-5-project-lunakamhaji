My Cats

Luna Kamhaji

I have two cats at home named Ash and Max. Ash is 6 months old, and Max is 5 years old.
Until last spring, we had a black cat named Zoro who passed away from lymphoma.
Due to his death, Max unfortunately fell into a depressive state where he would refuse to eat food.
Seeing this, we made the difficult decision to adopt a kitten in order for Max to have company.

Ash

We adopted my kitten Ash when he was around 1 month old. His litter was found abandoned by their feral mother
in a construction site by a young couple in Brooklyn.

Although he was a bit timid when he first arrived at our house, he has now grown to be a friendly, agile, and playful cat who enjoys the presence of people.
He eats almost everything, to the point where I can't leave food out around him.

Max

We adopted Max from a cat cafe in Brooklyn sometime in 2019 when he was around one year old.
He was very playful and curious, but when my other cat Zoro passed away, Max was very distraught and started to refuse any food and only wanted to sleep.
After we adopted Ash, he has become a bit more energetic.
